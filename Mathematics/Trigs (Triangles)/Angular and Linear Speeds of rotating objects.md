Arc length formula
$$
\begin{align}
s&={\theta \over 2\pi}2\pi r = \theta r \\ \\
v&={\text{arc length} \over t}= {\theta r \over t} \\ \\
\omega &= {\theta \over t} &&\omega \cancel{\propto} r\\ \\
v&=\omega r \\
\end{align}
$$

Area of sector of circle:
$$
A={\theta \over 2\pi} \pi r^2={1 \over 2}\theta r^2
$$
